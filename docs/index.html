Music Practice & Sheet Music Organizer

A powerful cross-platform application for musicians, dancers, magicians, and performers. Manage your repertoire, track practice sessions, and organize all your media in one beautiful place.

Powerful Features

📚 Repertoire Library

View your entire collection in a beautiful grid or list format. Organize pieces with custom groups and tags for easy access.

🎵 Detailed Piece View

Each piece has a dedicated page with comprehensive metadata including title, composer, difficulty, and custom tags.

📎 Flexible Media Attachments

Attach PDFs, Markdown notes, images, audio files, and video links. All your materials in one place.

⏱️ Practice Tracking

Log practice sessions, track dates, and monitor your progress with detailed statistics and history.

🔍 Smart Search & Filtering

Full-text search with advanced filtering by genre, difficulty, tags, practice history, and more.

💾 Backup & Restore

Export your entire library for safekeeping. Automatic periodic backups ensure your data is always protected.
